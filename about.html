My Story

Hello! I’m Jeffrey, an experienced Graphic Designer and visual communicator seeking to build a career as a UI designer. Since the Covid lock-down I have dedicated my time to the CareerFoundry UI certification course and have spent over 900 hours learning and building several UI projects. I offer solid visual design skills which are rooted in user-centric thinking, simplicity, and functionality. I also offer strong communication skills from my hospitality background which effectively allows me to communicate with clients, coworkers, and employees about their design requirements. I aim to be a team-oriented designer who creates effective solutions with inspiring visual designs. I am passionate about art, design, nature, and sports. When I’m not working I’m usually backpacking, playing hockey, or trying to improve my skills in one of my many hobbies.
